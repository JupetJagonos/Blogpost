    • Pango't Kayumanggi; Phillippines streets

    by Jupet Jagonos

    A Story telling gallery of every hard working Filipino from the streets of the Philippines. “Pango at Kayumanggi” is the basic definition of a Filipino that best describe them. This a collective-street-portrait photography that has been saved and gate-keep through out the years. As part of his self-training and discipline, Jupet photograph moments, store it to a drive and check it after a year or two. he believes that “Great photos are like vino - you know when it’s exceptional when you look at it after years of keeping (most of the time forgetting), it will still give you the chills when you have it”

    “Capturing a single moment is like making an imprint on history — You freeze time perfectly and that no one can experience it yet you let them see what you’ve experience”
