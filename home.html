    Creative collectives by Jupet Jagonos

    Jupet Jagonos a southeast asian photographer now based in Toronto. His orthodox style hone from the street photography where he believes that no the same photo are taken; The money shot — right place at the right moment. Jupet rebrand himself “almost” celebrating a new start here in Toronto while incapsulating his could’ve beens in the past and “Pedro” as a respect to his father’s dreams and plan.

    WHAT IS YOUR ALMOST?

    I stumble on this one liner in past that says “the most painful thing or word in our existence is the word almost.” how you define your almost is how you represent yourself. almost hit the jackpot, or im almost 100%, almost die today. almost made it out alive. these are two different things. and you define it your own. welcome to my almost. a visual story telling of my creative journey as I took a leap in following my dreams and passion, and leaving behind the life that was molded and prepared for me by my dad. hence, almost|pedro.
